Crystals Collector Game!

How to Play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will adda specific amout of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number. If your total score goes above the random number, you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, it will change the values of each crystal.
